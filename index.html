Side Projects

NHL Fantasy Tableau Dashboard
This is a Tableau dashboard of my current NHL fantasy hockey team and crucial statistics that can help inform me on the performance and value of my players. I extracted the data such that it included player's statistics (goals, assists, TOI, Shot percentage, etc.) from 2016, 2017 and 2018. I then created the dashboard that could filter depending on which player is chosen, using the filter bar on the right. This would indicate the location of the team that they play in on the map, and it will also highlight their statisitics accordingly. Including goals in the 2016-2018, proportion to all my players and the distribution of their points in terms of goals and assists.
